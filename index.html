Travel guide

  1. The closest airport with direct flights from the U.S is Frankfurt Airport in Germany ( Airport Code: FRA ). We recommend renting a car from Frankfurt to the wedding venue in Hambach, France. The drive is about 2 hours.
  2. You could also fly to Paris, and then take a train to Forbach, France, which is close to the wedding venue. The train ride from Paris is about 1hr 30 min.

What's around

  1. Strasbourg (1hr drive from wedding venue) Strasbourg is the capital of the Alsace region of France and is famous for its beautiful historical center, cathedral, local food specialties and places to shop. Strasbourg
  2. Colmar region (1hr 30 min drive) Take a trip to the Alsace Wine Route, to visit many of the charming historical towns such as Colmar, Riquewihr and Ribeauville and treat yourself to a wine tasting. Local vineyards specialize in Riesling and Gewürztraminer wines. Colmar region
  3. Saarland Therme (20 min drive) Make sure to take a few hours to visit the Saarland Therme bathhouse, at the German border. This place is amazingly relaxing and the Moroccan-inspired decor creates a very nice atmosphere. Open every day from 9:00 a.m. to midnight. Saarland Therme
  4. Völklinger Hütte (40 min drive) The Völklingen Ironworks (German: Völklinger Hütte) is located in the German town of Völklingen, Saarland. In 1994, it was declared by UNESCO as a World Heritage site. Today, the Völklinger Hütte is a museum. It currently hosts the world's largest exhibition of street art, The UrbanArt Biennale. Völklinger Hütte
  5. Paris (1hr 30 min train ride) Paris, France's capital, is a major European city and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy and culture. Paris

Other nearby cities

  1. You can fly to all the major European cities from Frankfurt-Hahn airport (HHN airport code). This airport only offers low-cost flights, so you can get anywhere in Europe for really cheap.
